About Capstone DevOps

UNA Technology

Capstone

DevOps

Students in any one of The University of North Alabama’s Computer Science & Information System programs - B.A. in CIS, B.S. in IT, B.S. in CS - that enroll in CIS 486: Projects in Information Systems adopt Agile System Development Practices (see agile manifesto) in pursuit of building and running a full-fledged DevOps environment.

Student Engagement
Students engage in a multitude of diverse development tasks and projects and around 2019 moved from soliciting community-sponsored projects to adopting JOBIE as the class pet-project. Well, the class didn’t really have a choice. Dr. Cumbie will have to elaborate on this one here - a single, strong-partnership project allows for controlling variability in student experience and avoids ethical complications of the “turn over the keys” student-consultancy model.
